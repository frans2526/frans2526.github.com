Sorry for the delay but I was really busy :-p I continue where I stopped so my first day of school I met some people but especially two who now are good friends Marie and Adam. Let’s Go

Sep 11th, 2012

Hello, I leave Brussels the 5 september to Vancouver for one year. The flight to Montreal was good and when I arrived and passed the border. I went to the immigration office and a men ask me with enthusiasm “Pourquoi venez au Canada aujourd’hui ?”. I was really suprise but in the good way of course. After I toke my Work & Holiday Visa and my second fligth. I sleept all the fligth to Vancouver so that was pretty cool when I arrived in Vancouver I was really tired yes it’s true guys :-p so I sleept all the night ;-)

The day after I received all my documents from the agency and I toke my Social Insurance Number for working in Canada. After I had gone to my first homestay but unfortunately I missed my homestay mum in the station so I walked between the Nanaimo station and my homestay. When I arrived there, there had nobody so I waited during 20 minutes outisde before they came back from a soccer game :-D but that was a sunny day so that was not really a big problem. My room was huge with a TV and the familly is really friendly. In the family there are the mum Kathie, the french father Patrick and they’re 3 kids Nik, Julian and Stefan. There is also an other student from Brazil Jaime. Everybody is so friendly, it’s awesome !

After I had been to a swimming pool with Jaime, Stefan and Stefan friends. That was really cool because I had talked with Jaime and we went to the steam and in the hot tub. The weekend finished, I went to the school “Tamwood Internationnal” when I met a lot of new friends from all around the world but especially Brazil, Japon and Spain (Catalogna(for you gyus :-D)). I think it’s really great to meet ohter people and know the new culture.
