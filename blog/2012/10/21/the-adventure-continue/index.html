The Adventure Continue

Oct 21st, 2012

Hi guys !

Sorry for the delay but I was really busy :-p I continue where I stopped so my first day of school I met some people but especially two who now are good friends Marie and Adam. Marie comes from Belgium and Adam comes from Barcelona. I don’t remember all the activity we did but one of my typical day in Tamwood was when I finished the school at 1:50pm Adam and I we visited some places of Vancouver with the sunshine :-p I leaved my first homestay after 3 weeks because I had extended for two more weeks in Tamwood so they didn’t keep me more longer so now I’m in a homesaty with two italian retired people, the homestay mum kook very well, she kooks every days pasta.

After 4 weeks there I needed to say goodbye of this amazing place and I started a marketing intership at Wishpond, it’s a startup in the downtown of Vancouver and for the moment I working there. I found an apartment with Adam in Vancouver but not in the downtown, it’s a quite place near from the public transports(+-20 min to the downtown). And because when I don’t know what I can say I will talk about the weather :-D so here in Vancouver, all the month of September was incredible hot and sunny but now the weather change to “Raincouver” and the temperature is decrease each day. This weekend they starting to snow in Alberta, in the north and the east of the British Columbia.

The Next week we will celebrate Halloween and maybe we will go to a haunted house.

See you later and don’t hesitate to write a comment,

Bye

NB: it’s time for me to watching the Walking Dead on AMC :-D
