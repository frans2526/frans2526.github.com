3 Months

Dec 9th, 2012

Hi guys,

It’s already 3 months I’m in Vancouver! That start to be long but I have a lot of work so I didn’t see all the time I spend here :-) I find a job in Wishpond where I’m Web Developer.
When I arrived in October there was only 15 people and now I think we arrive at least 35 person! It’s like my second family, we hang out every week to the WhiteSpot and it’s always a good time there :-)

What’s append between my last post and now?
A lot of party and hours of work :-p but except that some of my best left Vancouver to go back on there country so I’m sad for that and also because it’s the first time for me I will don’t pass chrismas with my family. The last Sunday I went to the Chrismas Parade with some friends that was really great and it’s really tipical to see all the people parading in the street, it’s like in the american movie. (I post some pictures at the end of the post)

Yesterday I went to Deep Cove because I never been there and also that was a beautiful day, so we were walking in the forest to the view point where we can see Deep Cove and in the back Vancouver. We saw also the snow on the top of the mountain that’s great because it’s something that I miss, it’s the cold and the snow especially because the temperature here it’s between 7C and 4C daily with rain the sunset it’s at 4:00pm and the sunrise at 7:20am.

Chrismas is there !
Here the Chrismas is everywhere and also earlier than in Belgium after Halloween it’s directly Christmas… Buisness is Buisness. My boss will organise a Christmas diner the next sunday for the company like in the american movie :-p

See you later and don’t hesitate to write a comment,
