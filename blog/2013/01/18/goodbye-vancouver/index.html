Goodbye Vancouver

Jan 18th, 2013

After less than four months in Vancouver, I have to say goodbye to all my new friends that I met during my trip in Van. It’s really difficult for me to do that because I met so many amazing people here but I want to move somewhere else.
Somewhere, where I can see some snow and not all this rain everyday… (I’m in Canada not in Belgium :-D)
So I decide to go first in Toronto to see a canadian friend but before I had to do my farewell party in the Pint :-p This name will be unforgettable for all my life.

Afterwards I want to go in Montreal or Quebec but I don’t know yet… I like speaking in english and the english culture but I think that it will be easier for me to find a job in Quebec where I can speak french.

Unfortunately, I don’t keep only a good memories of Van, this reason was my job in Wishpond, after only one month, I had already a bad impression of this place. My co-workers were funny and wonderful but the direction was totaly awfull and disrespectful.
We were at least between 40 and 45 persons in a small office, I had the habit to say to my colleague that we were in “Auschwitz”. Imagine to work in a subway, it’s the same but that was not the worst, they wanted always more “money” (for the Belgium people see the Luminis ads) and also I learned nothing except maybe “how you don’t have to manage a company” :-D
Just to remind you they didn’t pay us for the work that we did there…

Now it’s the past lets go Toronto!
